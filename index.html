About me

Currently, I am a Ph. D. student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Beijing Jiaotong University.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Machine Translation, and Large Language Models in the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). I welcome discussions with anyone interested in linguistics.
